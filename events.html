Downtown Los Altos Farmers' Market

The weekly market on State Street — fresh produce, prepared foods, flowers, live music, and the LAVA wine booth. Bring a tote.

5:00 – 8:00 pm

Ride downtown, dine downtown. Restaurants offer specials for cyclists, and the streets get a bit quieter for the evening.

47th Annual Los Altos Arts & Wine Festival

July 11 – July 12 · All weekend

The signature summer event. Over 200 artists, dozens of wineries, live music across the festival grounds, and food from local restaurants. The proceeds fund LAVA's beautification and community work all year.

Boots, brims, and brown spirits. A guided stroll through participating downtown venues with whiskey tastings paired with local bites.
