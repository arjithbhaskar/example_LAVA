Membership Benefits

What you get when you show up.

Membership is voluntary and helps fund efforts to promote the downtown through local advertising, events, and community giving. Here's what your dues unlock.

Directory Listing

Free listing in our member directory, including web address, phone, and category.

Event Postings

Complimentary postings of business-related events on the LAVA calendar and social media.

Sponsor Discounts

Reduced rates on sponsorship and advertising at signature LAVA events.

Networking

Educational meetings and social events with fellow merchants, property owners, and residents.

Voting Rights

Full voting rights in the association and eligibility for Board of Directors elections.

Committee Work

Contribute talent and expertise on working committees that shape downtown's future.

LAVA membership is open to businesses in the Downtown triangle (San Antonio Rd / Foothill Expwy / Edith Ave), companies looking to do business in Downtown, and residents of Los Altos.

Not Ready to Join Yet?

You can still give back.

Whether you're a member, a visitor, or a long-time local — a charitable gift to the Los Altos Village Fund supports the same events, lights, and flower pots you've come to love.
