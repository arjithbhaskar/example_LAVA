Where Your Gift Goes

Every dollar walks these streets

LAVA is a volunteer-driven, member-funded organization. Donations are pooled with membership dues and event proceeds to fund the year-round care of our six-block downtown.

3,200+ String Lights

Wrapped around every street tree along Main and State Streets — lit nightly, year-round, replaced and repaired by LAVA.

85 Flower Pots

Seasonal plantings refreshed four times a year at corners, crosswalks, and storefronts throughout the downtown triangle.

36+ Free Events

The Farmers' Market, Bike n' Dine, Holiday Stroll, Tree Lighting, Easter Egg Party — open to all, funded by gifts like yours.
